Creating a Symbiotic Relationship

Recon is an application that provides an efficient platform for businesses and customers to get to know each other on a better basis. Users provide businesses with valuable information concerning their daily products from which the businesses can take crucial decisions. Customers gain coupons and other incentives for their time playing a very intuitive game that is meant to help businesses gain information. This symbiotic relationship is what Recon seeks to nurture to spur a revolution in the business analytics/customer relations industry.

So how does Recon work?
Gaming
An interactive way for users to learn more about businesses and provide critical feedback
Business Analytics
Businesses can analyze customer data with demographics and such to really learn how to address their market
Discounts
Customers get coupons or other benefits for participation
