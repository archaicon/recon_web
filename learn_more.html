What's Recon

Recon is an application that provides an efficient platform for businesses and customers to get to know each other on a better basis. Users provide businesses with valuable information concerning their daily products from which the businesses can take crucial decisions. Customers gain coupons and other incentives for their time playing a very intuitive game that is meant to help businesses gain information
